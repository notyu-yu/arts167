Seriality Examples

Hello everyone, here are my 3 things that have some sense of seriality:

  1. The Cicada 3301 Puzzle series, where the next puzzle is hidden as the solution of the previous one. (Article if you are interested: https://www.boxentriq.com/code-breaking/cicada-3301-first-puzzle-walkthrough)

  2. Alternate reality game TheSunVanished, a series of Twitter posts about an apocalyptic world where the sun is gone and unknown creatures are lurking around. (https://twitter.com/thesunvanished?lang=en)

  3. Morning Corgi, a text service that sends pictures of corgis every day to people who have been signed up. (https://www.morningcorgi.com/")
